India’s Most Comprehensive Elderly Care Service!

Khyaal is an elderly care service that is focused towards making the lives of the elderly more comfortable by assisting in meeting their various needs like paying bills, buying grocery, providing emotional support, ensuring timely medication, booking appointments among many others.

How it works


Step 1

A Khyaal sales representative will call you and introduce the services of Khyaal.

Step 2

Once you have expressed your interest in Khyaal, a call will be scheduled with our sales representative and a Khyaal Caregiver to understand your requirement.

Step 3

We will setup a call between your elderly loved one and the assigned Khyaal Caregiver to understand from them about their specific needs.

Step 4

The assigned caregiver will begin interacting with your elderly loved one over WhatsApp and will also share a dedicated phone number with the elderly to place their requests.

Step 5

We will regularly monitor various aspects of your elderly loved one and share a report with you on a weekly basis.
